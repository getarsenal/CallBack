Privacy Policy — Callback

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Callback is a personal job-interview tracking app. We designed it to keep your data in your hands. This policy explains what the app stores and where it goes.

What the app stores

Callback lets you record information about your job search: companies, roles, interview rounds, the people you meet, your notes, compensation details, job descriptions, and an optional copy of your résumé. You enter all of this yourself.

Where your data lives

  • On your device. By default, everything is stored locally on the device using your browser/app local storage. It never leaves your device unless you turn on Cloud Sync.
  • Optional Cloud Sync. If you choose to enable sync, your data is sent to a database that you create and control (a Supabase project you set up with your own account and keys). Callback has no servers of its own and never receives, stores, or sees your data. Sync traffic goes directly from your device to your database.

What we collect

Nothing. Callback contains no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs that collect personal information. The developer does not have access to your data.

Third-party services

  • Company logos: the app may request a company's favicon from Google's public favicon service using a website domain you entered. No personal data is sent.
  • Supabase (only if you enable Cloud Sync, and only to the project you created).
  • AI prompt copying: the app generates text for you to paste into an AI assistant of your choice. Callback does not send anything to any AI service itself.

Deleting your data

You can edit or delete any item at any time inside the app, or export a backup file. If you use Cloud Sync, deleting the Supabase project removes the cloud copy. Removing the app removes the local copy.

Children

Callback is intended for adults managing their own job search and is not directed at children.
